Ship sprites in this directory are derived from the Space Trader Windows
port (SpaceTraderSource201) by Jay French and David Pierron, itself a
port of the original Palm OS Space Trader by Pieter Spronck.

Original source: https://www.spronck.net/spacetrader.html
License: GNU General Public License v2 or later

The bitmaps were converted from BMP to PNG and the original white
background was alpha-keyed to transparent. No artistic changes were made.
